Visiting Our Church

We meet Sunday mornings at 10:00 am in the Blacksburg Community Center at 725 Patrick Henry Drive in Blacksburg, VA. Please feel welcome to join us for lunch immediately following the service.

What To Expect

We are a church plant made up of those of various ages and backgrounds. You will find that our services consist of Scripture readings, worship music that includes contemporary and traditional songs, prayer, and preaching. Preaching is central to our service, so you will hear Bible-based sermons that average 45 minutes. Wondering how to dress? You will fit in whether you dress more formally or casually. Come as you are! Wondering what to do about your kids? At ekklesia, you will find many children sitting in the service with their parents, but a nursery is also available with audio of the service. Lunch afterwards is a sandwich buffet and we hope you will stay and eat so we can get to know you and you us. It is free, and you should feel no need to provide anything. We hope to see you Sunday!
