Visiting Our Church

We meet Sunday mornings at 10:00 am in the Blacksburg Community Center at 725 Patrick Henry Drive in Blacksburg, VA. Please feel welcome to join us for lunch immediately following the service.

What To Expect

We are a small, close-knit church made up of local families and students meeting in the Blacksburg Community Center. Our service runs from 10:00 - 11:45 and is centered on a Biblical sermon that lasts about 45 minutes. Dress is casual. We have many children and all are welcome in our services, though a nursery is available for those who need it. Lunch is provided after the service and is a wonderful time of fellowship with one another and our visitors. Come enjoy our great God with us!
